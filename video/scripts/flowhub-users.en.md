# FlowHub — Explainer Video (End User)

<!-- scene: hook -->
A note here, a link there, a task somewhere else. All day long, little bits of information pile up in all kinds of places.

<!-- scene: problem -->
And then the real work begins: sorting, copying, pasting — from one app into the next. It costs you time and patience.

<!-- scene: solution -->
FlowHub puts an end to that. You drop everything into a single inbox — and FlowHub takes care of the rest.

<!-- scene: demo -->
Type "Inception — watch again", and it lands as a card in your movie list. Send an article link, and it waits in your reading list.

<!-- scene: benefits -->
For you that means less copy-paste, time saved, and everything automatically in the right place — fully self-hosted and private.

<!-- scene: close -->
FlowHub — your smart inbox.
